Workshops on Basics of Computer

Society conducted a workshop on THE BASICS OF COMPUTER FOR FIRST AND SECOND YEAR students of the institute. we covered topics like different applications of Microsoft Package (mainly WORD ,POWERPOINT , EXCEL) ; DBMS , MS ACCESS ; COMPUTER ARCHITECTURE , CLASSIFICATION AND GENERATIONS. Event was conducted at IT CONFERENCE HALL , IT BLOCK.

Blur Racing

Excursion To Mussorie

All the arrangement were made prior in hand and the society looks forward to plan more trips in future.
